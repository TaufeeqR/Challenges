Butter Chicken Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6-8 Chicken Fillet Breast(bite size)
  • 2 Small Onions(chopped finely)
  • 3 Tablespoons Garlic and Ginger(peeled/grated)
  • 1 cup Plain Yoghurt
  • 3 Tablespoons Roasted Masala
  • 2 Teaspoons of Jeera
  • 2 Tablespoons of Paprika
  • 1-2 Teaspoons Tumeric
  • 2 Teaspoons Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 cupCoconut Cream or Coconut Milk/li>
  • Bunch of chopped Coriander
  • 3 Tablespoons of Frying Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon Tomato Paste
  • Salt to Taste
  • 2 Whole Chillies(optional)
  • 1 Lemon(juiced)

Method

  1. In a bowl add your chicken, yoghurt, lemon juice, tomato paste, garlic and Ginger, all your spices, salt and whole chillies .
    Mix everything together with hand. Cover with the bowl clingwrap and let it marinade for a few hours or overnight.
  2. In a heated pot(high heat) add the oil and onions chopped finely, fry until golden brown
  3. Add a cup of warm water and cook the onions until all water has cooked away.
  4. Next add your chicken mix with any leftover marinade in bowl and add the marinade to the pot as well.
    Cook on medium heat stirring occasionally, cook until the chicken is tender, approximately 15-20 minutes, before serving.
  5. Add the cream and a bunch of coriander.
  6. Serve with White Basmati rice.
